Smart OPD

About Us

Smart OPD Management System is an intelligent digital platform designed to transform traditional outpatient department operations into a seamless and automated experience.

Our system dynamically manages patient tokens based on doctor availability, consultation time, and real-time session capacity to significantly reduce waiting time and overcrowding.

Developed by Team Avictron, Smart OPD focuses on innovation, efficiency, and patient satisfaction while creating smarter healthcare environments using modern technology.
